# Conscious Future Society — Project Brief

## Organization Identity
The Conscious Future Society (CFS) is a future-facing movement and organization at the intersection of:
- Consciousness & meditation
- Neuroscience & science
- Technology & innovation
- Leadership & societal transformation
- Spirituality & inner growth

**Website:** http://theconsciousfuturesociety.com/

---

## Core Repositioning Goal
The site is evolving FROM: a conference/event website
INTO: a premium platform and movement headquarters

1. **Homepage** → Central identity of CFS as an organization and movement
2. **TCFSC 2026** → New dedicated nav item/subpage for all conference content

---

## Emotional & Aesthetic Target
The site should feel like:
- A high-end research institute / modern think tank
- A cinematic spiritual-tech experience
- Apple-level polish mixed with consciousness exploration
- Premium future-facing consciousness platform

NOT like:
- A generic wellness or self-help brand
- A generic conference landing page
- A neon-heavy cyberpunk or Web3 project
- A trendy AI landing page

---

## Brand & Color System
- **Base:** deep navy / cosmic blue (#0a0e1a range)
- **Accent:** gold (#c9a84c range)
- **Highlight:** subtle blue illumination
- **Typography:** elegant, premium, minimal
- **Spacing:** generous — premium whitespace
- **Feel:** restrained futurism, not sci-fi excess

Avoid: neon-heavy palettes, oversaturated colors, excessive glassmorphism, generic glow aesthetics.

---

**vividand.co** is the primary structural and design reference.

You MAY closely follow:
- Overall layout structure and section pacing
- Whitespace usage and typography hierarchy
- Transition philosophy and interaction design
- Section composition and premium motion feel
- Modern immersive web experience patterns

Do NOT clone: branding, text content, logos, exact typography, exact animations, exact color palette, exact imagery.

---

## Hero Animation — DO NOT TOUCH
A custom cinematic hero animation already exists in this project. It is complete and must be preserved exactly.

**Animation concept:**
- Glowing spiritual/energetic humanoid being suspended in space
- Scroll-driven, pinned during animation
- As user scrolls: original being descends, illuminated clones ascend, each progressively brighter
- Final fully illuminated state transitions into next section
- Built with GSAP + ScrollTrigger
- Visual language: deep cosmic backgrounds, gold illumination, subtle blue accents

**Rules:**
- DO NOT redesign, replace, or modify the hero animation
- DO NOT add conflicting visuals in the hero zone
- DO NOT tightly couple hero text to static layout
- Design all sections to feel like a natural continuation of this hero
- The hero animation is the most visually dominant motion system on the site
