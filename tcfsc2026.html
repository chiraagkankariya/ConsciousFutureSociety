About TCFSC 2026

Where Depth
Meets Direction

The Conscious Future Society Conference is an annual gathering for leaders, researchers, practitioners, and thinkers who believe that the future of civilisation depends on the evolution of human consciousness.


TCFSC 2026 brings together voices from neuroscience, meditation, technology, leadership, and the arts — not for surface-level inspiration, but for rigorous, honest conversation about what it means to build a more conscious world.
